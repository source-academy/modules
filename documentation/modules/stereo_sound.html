    Module stereo_sound

    The stereo_sound module build on the sound module by accommodating stereo sounds. Within this module, all Sounds are represented in stereo, with two waves, left and right.

    A Stereo Sound (just denoted as "Sound" in this document) is a pair(pair(left_wave, right_wave), duration) where duration is the length of the Sound in seconds. The constructor make_stereo_sound and accessors get_left_wave, get_right_wave, and get_duration are provided. The make_sound constructor from sounds is syntatic sugar for make_stereo_sounds with equal waves.
